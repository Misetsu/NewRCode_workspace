NewRCode

NewR-Code is a service that synthesizes QR code and images.
You can synthesize a QR code with an image of your choice. Unlike conventional QR codes,
you can decorate and make your QR image more stylish and easier to distinguish.

  3. Send URL text
  4. Send an image
